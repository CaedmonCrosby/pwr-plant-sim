PWR MCR • Panel Bodies (Realistic Horseshoe)

Controls

  • Click canvas to lock pointer (FPS mode)
  • WASD: Walk around the control room
  • Mouse: Look around
  • Shift: Run (faster movement)
  • H: Toggle this help
  • ESC: Unlock pointer

Panels (bodies only - structural design)

  • Center (Reactor Control): Main rod control area
  • Left wing: RCS (Reactor Coolant System)
  • Far left: ESF (Engineered Safety Features)
  • Right wing: Steam Generator & Feedwater
  • Far right: Turbine Generator
  • Right side bay: Electrical Distribution
  • Central console: Plant Process Computer / HMI

This is a focused realistic representation of Westinghouse 4-loop PWR Main Control Board (horseshoe MCB) panel bodies. Panels are placed tightly against each other and the back wall for authentic continuous control board appearance. Walk close to inspect construction details (benches, vertical sections, rivets, conduits, labels, etc.).

More features (interactions, alarms, sim) can be added later on these exact bodies.
